Research Projects

Current Research

MARINA-Plastic Retention Optimization

Master's thesis research on optimizing plastic retention rates in water systems, focusing on urban environmental management and global change impacts. Broad topic covering water, urban, BGI, and NBS (specific topic to be confirmed Sep 2025).

DOC Research in Lapland

Research Assistant at Eastern Finland University. Conducted field samplings in Lapland including DOC (Dissolved Organic Carbon) emissions from the Kemi River, performed spectral measurements and water incubations, and analyzed field samples using GIS. Results expected to be published in January 2026.

Urban Forestry Research

Urban Tree Inventory Analysis

Analyzed over 130,000 City of Surrey tree inventory data to identify patterns in tree location, mortality, species distribution, and lifespan. Used advanced statistical methods to understand urban forest dynamics.

Land Surface Temperature Analysis

Investigated the impact of urban structure on surface temperature at UBC (2014-2022) using 30m resolution mapping and random forest modeling. Both personal and group projects focusing on urban heat island effects.

Pollinators and Climate Change Study

Field survey of insects and bumble bees, focusing on their interactive relationship with plants within 18 urban parks in Vancouver. Research on pollinator behavior and climate change impacts in urban environments.

Environmental Microbiology

Phosphorus-Solubilizing Microorganism Interactions

Conducted research on interactions between phosphorus-solubilizing microorganism (PSM) strains and their effects on plant growth. Led team in proposal writing and project execution at State Key Laboratory of Subtropical Silviculture.

PSM and Phyllostachys edulis Interactions

Investigated interactions between phosphorus-solubilizing microorganisms and Phyllostachys edulis (bamboo). Responsible for field work and data analysis in forest ecosystem studies.

Leadership & Outreach Projects

World Without Borders Research

Led research team focusing on people's attitudes toward reintroducing plants and animals into campus environments. Online research project exploring community perspectives on biodiversity restoration.

Urban Forestry Database Creation

Volunteer research assistant gathering urban forestry documents for database creation. Contributed to building comprehensive resource library for urban forestry research and practice.
