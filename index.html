About Me

I am a passionate environmental researcher with a focus on water systems, global change, and sustainable urban development. Currently pursuing my Master's degree in Urban Environmental Management (Water System and Global Change) at Wageningen University & Research in the Netherlands.

My research interests span across multiple disciplines including water quality assessment, microplastics research, scenario analysis, environmental science, and climate change adaptation. I have conducted research in four different countries (Canada, Netherlands, Finland, and China), gaining diverse perspectives on environmental challenges and solutions.

I have extensive experience in field research, data analysis, and GIS applications. My work includes studies on MARINA-Plastic model enhancement, urban structure and surface temperature, pollinator-plant interactions, and water quality monitoring.
